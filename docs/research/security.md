# Security & OWASP Top 10

## Sources
- OWASP Top 10 project hub summarizing 2021 guidance and 2025 release candidate timeline. [Link](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/)
- OWASP Top 10:2025 RC1 site detailing updated categories and differences from 2021. [Link](https://owasp.org/Top10/)

## Notes
- 2021 Top 10 highlights risks including Broken Access Control, Cryptographic Failures, Injection, Insecure Design, Security Misconfiguration, Vulnerable and Outdated Components, Identification and Authentication Failures, Software and Data Integrity Failures, Logging and Monitoring Failures, and SSRF. [Link](https://owasp.org/Top10/)
- 2025 RC1 reorders risks and introduces Software Supply Chain Failures plus Mishandling of Exceptional Conditions while emphasizing modern AppSec programs. [Link](https://owasp.org/Top10/)
- OWASP urges teams to adopt the Top 10 as an awareness standard and contribute feedback on the 2025 RC announced November 6, 2025. [Link](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/)
